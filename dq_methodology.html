Methodology

The methodology for developing our Data Quality Assessment Framework encompasses a systematic approach structured in a number of phases. The phases include: Literature review: we conducted a literature review to gain understanding on the existing data quality methods, frameworks, and systems. Selection of data quality dimensions and metrics: This encompasses selecting dimensions and their corresponding metrics for evaluating the quality of open data. Discussions on best ways to define data quality dimensions and their associated metrics: Tool development: entails developing a tool for scoring data quality metrics.
